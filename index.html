Research Experiences

Thesis

Assessing University Students' Knowledge Attitudes, and Practice of Plagiarism in the Context of Detection Tools: an Emperical Study in a Developing Country Setting.

The primary objective of the present study is to assess Bangladeshi university students' knowledge, attitude and practice of plagiarism and awareness of plagiarism detection tools.

The study will provide insights into students' perceptions of plagiarism and plagiarism detection tools, including whether they view these tools as effective resisting or simply as barriers to be bypassed. Also, it will provide a framework for institutions to strengthen their approach to reducing plagiarism by combining detection tools with targeted awareness and educational initiatives.

Research Monograph

Generative AI: A Study on Ethical Factors, Mental Well-being and Future Dependency among University Students in Bangladesh

This study focuses on AI using the trend of Bangladesh university students in academic practice and their stance considering trust issues, ethical use, impact on mental health and well-being, and future dependency.

The study found, students view generative AI as a trustworthy tool, there are issues since they are unaware of its constraints and best practices. It becomes ever more clear that academic institutions need to have policies on AI usage. These policies provide a framework within which to handle issues, set limits, and direct students towards responsible and knowledgeable participation. Furthermore, guidelines designed specifically for the use of AI in academics can provide students with the information they need to use the technology successfully. Recognizing students' concerns about a possible reliance on AI for educational procedures highlights how crucial it is to promote a well-rounded and informed attitude. Education institutions may exploit the potential benefits of generative AI through proactive policymaking, which can mitigate possible dangers and foster creativity, critical thinking, and student well-being.
